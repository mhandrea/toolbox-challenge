This is a memory game. You have to find all 8 matches in order to win. Click on a tile, one at a time, until you have flipped two over. If they match, they stay face up and you solved a match. If the tiles did not match, they both will flip back over. Try to find all the matches with the fewest misses, and lowest time. Good luck!
